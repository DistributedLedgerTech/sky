Our Journey

The SkyKnet Story

2020

The Vision Takes Shape

SkyKnet (Distributed Ledger Technologies Inc.) was founded with a singular vision: to make blockchain technology accessible to everyone. Starting from a home office, the foundation was laid for what would become a comprehensive suite of enterprise-grade blockchain solutions.

2021

Knexcoin is Born

Recognizing the need for a scalable, energy-efficient blockchain, we developed Knexcoin—a Layer-1 blockchain powered by proof of bandwidth consensus. This breakthrough brought sustainable distributed ledger technology to developers and enterprises worldwide.

2022

Expanding the Ecosystem

With the success of Knexcoin, we expanded our portfolio to include KnexPay for payment processing and KnexWallet for secure cryptocurrency management. The ecosystem grew rapidly as enterprises adopted our infrastructure for real-world applications.

2023

Innovation Accelerates

KnexMail launched as our encrypted communication platform, while Superknet debuted as the definitive block explorer for the Knexcoin network. The Ayedex decentralized exchange brought new liquidity options and trustless trading to our growing ecosystem.

2024-25

Building Tomorrow

Today, DLT continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in blockchain technology. With multiple products serving users worldwide and a roadmap full of ambitious projects, we're just getting started on our mission to build the future of digital finance.
