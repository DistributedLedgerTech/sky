Network Activity Index

Counter-Cyclical Rewards: More in bear, less in bull.

NAI adjusts validator rewards inversely to network activity. Keeps validators online when it matters most — during low-activity periods.

Damping Factor

TARGET = 90-day rolling average NAI score CURRENT = Latest epoch NAI score Damping = clamp(TARGET / CURRENT, 0.25, 3.0) Bear market → activity LOW → damping 3.0× → 300% rewards Bull market → activity HIGH → damping 0.25× → 25% rewards Neutral → damping 1.0× → standard rewards
