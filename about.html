Our Journey

The DLT Story

2020

The Vision Takes Shape

Distributed Ledger Technologies was founded with a singular vision: to make blockchain technology accessible to everyone. Starting from a home office, the foundation was laid for what would become a comprehensive suite of enterprise-grade blockchain solutions.

2021

MacMetal Miner is Born

Recognizing the untapped potential of Apple Silicon for cryptocurrency mining, we developed MacMetal Miner—the first professional-grade Bitcoin mining application optimized for M-series chips. This breakthrough brought mining capabilities to millions of Mac users worldwide.

2022

Expanding the Ecosystem

With the success of MacMetal Miner, we expanded our portfolio to include Echelon for enterprise blockchain solutions and began development on revolutionary payment systems. The KNEX protocol emerged as our answer to sustainable, bandwidth-based consensus mechanisms.

2023

Innovation Accelerates

HydroDollar launched as our hydrogen-backed stablecoin initiative, while CORA revolutionized the gaming industry with Bitcoin-backed casino chips. The Ayedex decentralized exchange brought new liquidity options to the Stellar ecosystem.

2024-25

Building Tomorrow

Today, DLT continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in blockchain technology. With multiple products serving users worldwide and a roadmap full of ambitious projects, we're just getting started on our mission to build the future of digital finance.
