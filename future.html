1. Information We Collect

Distributed Ledger Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") operates CoraWallet, a Progressive Web Application for Stellar blockchain interactions. We collect the following types of information:

  • Local Storage Data: Encrypted wallet keys, addresses, transaction history, and user preferences stored locally on your device
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance metrics via Google Analytics
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and screen resolution for optimization
  • Network Data: IP addresses, connection timestamps, and Stellar network interaction logs for security
  • Camera Access: Temporary access for QR code scanning (no images stored)
  • Clipboard Access: For copying addresses and transaction data (user-initiated only)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing secure wallet functionality and transaction processing
  • Improving app performance and user experience

  • Providing customer support and technical assistance

3. Data Storage & Security

Your security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: Private keys and sensitive data remain on your device using browser local storage
  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your private keys or wallet data on our servers
  • HTTPS: All communications use secure HTTPS encryption
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments and updates

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except:

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cora Wallet

Is Cora Wallet safe to use?
Yes, Cora Wallet employs bank-grade security with client-side encryption. Your private keys never leave your device and are encrypted with AES-256. We use a zero-knowledge architecture, meaning we cannot access your funds even if we wanted to.
Do I need to download an app?
No downloads required! Cora is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that runs directly in your browser. You can add it to your home screen for a native app-like experience without using app store downloads.
What's the difference between Mainnet and Testnet?
Mainnet is the live Stellar network with real XLM that has monetary value. Testnet is a testing environment with free test XLM that has no value - perfect for learning and experimentation without risk.
How do I get started with Stellar?
Start by creating a wallet on Testnet to practice with free XLM. You can get test XLM from Friendbot instantly. Once comfortable, create a Mainnet wallet and fund it with real XLM from an exchange or another user.
What are the transaction fees?
Stellar has extremely low fees - typically around 0.00001 XLM per transaction (less than a penny). This makes it ideal for micropayments and frequent transactions.
Can I use Cora offline?
Yes! You can view your balance, transaction history, and manage accounts offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to send transactions or refresh your balance from the network.
What if I lose my private key?
If you lose your private key, there's no way to recover your wallet or funds. This is the nature of decentralized blockchain technology. Always backup your private key securely and never share it with anyone.
Is my data collected or shared?
We collect minimal analytics data to improve the app and ensure security. Your wallet data stays on your device. We never sell your data or share it with third parties except as required by law. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Who can I contact for support?
For support, bug reports, or questions, email us at admin@corawallet.com. We're here to help you have the best experience with Cora Wallet.
